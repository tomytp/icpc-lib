\subsection{Branching Factors}

The recurrence $T(n) = T(n-i) + T(n-j)$ is $\mathcal{O}(\tau(i, j)^n)$. Also, the recurrence $T(n) = T(n-i) + T(n-j) + f(n)$ is $\mathcal{O}(\tau(i, j)^n \cdot f(n))$.
