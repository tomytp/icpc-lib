\subsection{Bell Numbers} These count the number of ways to partition $[n]$ into subsets. They obey the recurrence:

\begin{equation*}
    \mathcal{B}_{n+1} = \sum_{k=0}^n \binom{n}{k} \mathcal{B}_k
\end{equation*}
